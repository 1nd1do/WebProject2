Historic Terms of the Treaty

            - Provide 160 acres of land for each family of five, or in proportion for smaller or larger families.
            - Maintain a school on each reserve.
            - Pay each family of five $15, or a proportional amount for larger or smaller families.
            - Set aside and reserve specific lots of land for the exclusive use of the First Nations inhabitants.
